How to Play
Crack the city in 8 tries

The Goal

Guess the mystery city in 8 attempts or fewer. Each guess reveals how close you are across 8 different categories.

Color Meanings

  • Green — Exact match with the target city
  • Yellow — Close or related (nearby region, similar climate, etc.)
  • Grey — No match

Arrows

For numerical categories (Population, Size, Avg Temp), an arrow guides you:

  • ↑ The answer is HIGHER than your guess
  • ↓ The answer is LOWER than your guess

Strategy Tip

Start broad — pick a city from a region you suspect, then narrow down using the feedback. Close matches mean you're on the right track.
